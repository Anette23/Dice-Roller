Dice Roller

How to play dice roller?

  1. Click the Roll Dice button
  2. When you get number 1, your score will be deleted and next player will play.
  3. You can clik Hold Score button
  4. Hold Score button holds your score and next player can play
  5. When you click on New Game button, the new game will start
